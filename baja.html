Design

Steering Rack Housing

I designed the steering rack housing for the 2023-2024 Season (SR24). The goal of this housing was to fit the new steering system. These few slides I made for our Detailed Design Review in November 2023 explain the differences between SR24 and SR23 (2022-2023 Season).



This project helped me start learning how to conduct finite element analysis (FEA) in SolidWorks. While teammates helped me set up part-level and assembly-level FEA, I wrote down some notes, which extended into notes throughout the entire rest of the design process of the steering rack housing and steering rack housing pillow block.
