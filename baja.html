Design

Steering Rack Housing

I designed the steering rack housing for the 2023-2024 season (SR24). The goal was to fit the new steering system. Previously, the tie rods were behind the spindle shafts, but this year we moved the tie rods to the front of the spindle shafts to put the tie rods in tension during wheel collisions.
