Design

Steering Rack Housing

I designed the steering rack housing for the 2023-2024 Season (SR24). The goal of this housing was to fit the new steering system. These few slides I made for our Detailed Design Review in November 2023 explain the differences between SR24 and SR23 (2022-2023 Season) and the benefits of the new design.


This project helped me start learning how to conduct finite element analysis (FEA) in SolidWorks. While teammates helped me set up part-level and assembly-level FEA, I took some notes, which extended into notes throughout the entire rest of the design process of the steering rack housing and steering rack housing pillow block.
